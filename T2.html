Course Syllabus

Dance Styles:

Foundation: Ballet (essential for technique, discipline). Popular Styles: Jazz, Tap, Hip Hop, Lyrical, Contemporary, Musical Theatre, Latin Fusion, Acro. Specialty: Modern, Creative Movement, Ballroom, Folk Dance.

Technique & Theory:

Ballet: Barre work, center work, pointe work (advanced). Dance Science: Kinesiology, nutrition, body alignment. History & Culture: Dance history, cultural context, professional repertoire. Music: Musicality, music theory, rhythm.
